Our Servitisation Framework

Central to our work is the Servitisation Framework — a maturity model that supports organisations as they evolve from Stage 1 (Product-Led) to Stage 5 (Service-Driven). We’ve identified 20 core Service responsibilities, weighted by organisation type, maturity stage and growth ambition. These sit within four key pillars:

Sales & Marketing

Revenue generation, customer engagement, solution positioning.

Delivery & Operations

Maintenance execution, technical support, field service performance.

Governance, Risk & Performance Excellence

Financial integrity, SLA management, margin discipline, operational risk.

Innovation & Development

Service development, digital enablement, predictive/remote capability.
